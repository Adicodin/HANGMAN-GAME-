RULES
In this game there will be a word hidden with blanks related to Physics, Chemistry and Computer Science.
You have to guess letters to guess the word correctly. You have 10 lives. You have to guess the word and
you can use a hint but remember that hint will activate only if there are 3 or less lives left and if you
ask for hint your one life will be lost.

There are two ways to play this game:

DEFAULT
In this, you will get the default words which are
already present in the game.

MULTIUSER
In this, multiple players can play the game. One can enter
the word which he/she wants the other one to guess and the
other player can guess the word entered by the player one.

So to play the multiuser game, click on the multiuser button. If you want to play the normal game, click on
the default game button.
